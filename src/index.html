Info

Tony Christopher is a designer, creative director and creative technologist working in three dimensions.

Originally from upstate New York, Steve studied industrial design at Rochester Institute of Technology with a focus on exhibits and environments. He's driven by curiosity, active learning, an iterative approach to establishing and perfecting processes, and early adoption of promising technologies. These core values support a guiding principle that aesthetic and functional ambitions need the technical knowledge to accomplish them.

Steve started his career by interpreting and presenting knowledge as built environments. He works to bring the same sensibilities to spatial computing and immersive digital experiences. His enthusiastic following of emerging technologies allows an understanding of the continuities and transitions between physical and digital experiences.
